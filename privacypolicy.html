Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

Drama Asia ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our content management system and related services.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account with Drama Asia, we collect your name, email address, and other information you provide during registration.

TikTok Integration Data: Through the TikTok Login Kit, we collect your TikTok profile information, user ID, and authentication tokens necessary for accessing the TikTok Content Posting API.

Content Data: We collect and store the drama videos, metadata, descriptions, and scheduling information you upload to our platform.

Usage Data: We collect information about how you use Drama Asia, including login times, features used, and performance metrics.

Device Information: We automatically collect device information, IP address, browser type, and operating system for service optimization and security.

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision: To provide and maintain the Drama Asia content management system, including automated video scheduling and publishing features.

TikTok Integration: To authenticate with TikTok and publish content on your behalf using the TikTok Content Posting API.

Analytics and Improvement: To analyze service usage, improve our features, and optimize performance.

Communication: To send you service updates, security alerts, and support communications.

Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and ensure service security.

TikTok API Integration

Drama Asia integrates with TikTok's official APIs to provide seamless content publishing:

Login Kit: We use TikTok Login Kit for secure authentication. When you log in with TikTok, we receive access tokens and basic profile information necessary for account linking.

Content Posting API: We use the TikTok Content Posting API to publish videos to your TikTok account based on your scheduling preferences. You maintain full control over what content is published.

Data Sharing with TikTok: Content you choose to publish through Drama Asia is shared with TikTok's servers for posting to your account. We do not share your personal data with TikTok beyond what is required for API functionality.

API Permissions: We only request the minimum permissions necessary for our service to function. You can revoke access at any time through your TikTok account settings.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and at rest using AES-256 encryption.

Access Control: Strict access controls and authentication mechanisms prevent unauthorized access to your data.

Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments.

Secure Storage: Your content and personal information are stored in secure, SOC 2 compliant data centers.

Data Retention

Account Data: We retain your account information while your account is active. Upon account deletion, we delete personal information within 30 days, except as required by law.

Content Data: Your video content and metadata are retained until you delete them or close your account.

Analytics Data: Usage analytics are retained in anonymized form for service improvement purposes.

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Correction: You can access and update your account information through your Drama Asia dashboard.

Data Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data at any time through the account settings or by contacting us.

TikTok Integration: You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time, which will revoke our access to publish content on your behalf.

Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect service functionality.

Third-Party Services

Drama Asia may use third-party services for:

Analytics: We may use analytics services to understand how our platform is used.

Support: Customer support tools may be used to provide assistance.

Infrastructure: Cloud hosting and content delivery networks for service reliability.

We carefully select partners who comply with applicable data protection laws and require them to protect your information.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable laws.

Children's Privacy

Drama Asia is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Material changes will be communicated through email or in-app notifications.
